iPhone Instructions

Follow these steps to complete the setup on your Apple device:

  1. Open the Settings app from your home screen.
  2. Scroll down and tap on General.
  3. Select the configuration option and toggle it to On.
  4. Click the button below to verify your connection.

Android Instructions

Follow these steps to complete the setup on your Android device:

  1. Swipe down from the top of your screen to open Quick Settings.
  2. Tap the Gear icon to enter standard settings.
  3. Navigate to Connections and enable the configuration.
  4. Click the button below to verify your connection.
