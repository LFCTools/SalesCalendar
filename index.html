Sync Our Calendars

Select the schedules you want to sync directly to your device. You'll get automatic updates for date and time changes.

How to Sync

🍎 iPhone / Apple Users:
Tap the dark button and select "Subscribe" when the prompt appears.

🤖 Android / Google Users:
Requires a Google/Gmail account. Tap the blue button. When the webpage opens, look for the "+ Google Calendar" button in the bottom right corner to add it to your account. Note: Once added, check your calendar app's settings menu to make sure the calendar is checked/turned on, as it often defaults to hidden!
